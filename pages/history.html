August 3, 1910

Hydro's Juice Store Grand Opening

Hydro's Juice Store proudly opened its doors to the public on August 3, 1910, led by our visionary founder, Hydro. From the start, Hydro was dedicated to providing unique, high-quality juice blends crafted from locally sourced fruits. His passion for flavor and freshness quickly earned the store a loyal customer base, setting the foundation for over a century of success.

Expansion into Exotic Juices

As demand grew, Hydro sought to bring more unique flavors to our customers. On March 15, 1925, we introduced a line of juices made from rare, exotic fruits sourced from around the world. Hydro’s commitment to offering one-of-a-kind tastes elevated the store’s reputation, making it a beloved destination for juice lovers near and far.

March 15, 1925
March 15, 1925

The "Essence of Life" Juice Launch

Hydro's Juice Store hit a major milestone on June 21, 1940, with the release of our famous “Essence of Life” juice blend. Customers praised its rejuvenating properties, and it quickly became one of our most popular offerings. This blend solidified our status as a health-conscious brand that focuses on wellness and vitality.

Introduction of Dark Juices

After a brief, unexplained closure following a local power outage on November 7, 1952, Hydro’s Juice Store reopened with a new line of dark-colored juice blends. These new offerings provided a bold twist on our classic recipes and became a hit with adventurous customers looking for something different. These blends have continued to be a part of our unique product lineup.

November 7, 1952
September 9, 1968

Hydro's Legacy Continues

On September 9, 1968, our founder, Hydro, made the decision to step away from daily operations, leaving behind a legacy of passion and innovation. Though he left without fanfare, his vision remains alive and well in every juice blend we create today. The store has been under careful stewardship ever since, with a focus on honoring Hydro’s original principles.

Return of the "Elderberry Blend"

After years of customer requests, we were proud to reintroduce the popular “Elderberry Blend” on October 13, 1980. The blend, known for its rich, full-bodied flavor, quickly regained its status as a fan favorite. Its timeless recipe has been lovingly recreated to match the original, bringing joy to a new generation of customers.

October 13, 1980
April 4, 2005

Global Recognition

Hydro's Juice Store received international acclaim on April 4, 2005, when we were featured in a prominent global magazine. This recognition brought our humble store to a worldwide audience, cementing our reputation as a purveyor of distinctive, high-quality juices. We are honored to share our history and unique flavors with juice lovers from around the globe.

Launch of the Limited-Edition "Moonshade Nectar"

On January 31, 2020, under a rare red moon, we introduced a special, limited-edition juice blend called “Moonshade Nectar.” This exclusive offering was available for just one day and sold out immediately. Though it was a short-lived promotion, the blend left a lasting impression on those lucky enough to try it, highlighting our commitment to offering bold, unforgettable experiences.

January 31, 2020
June 5, 2023

New Flagship Store Opening

As demand continued to grow, we proudly opened a new flagship store on June 5, 2023. This modern location features an expanded menu of fresh juice blends, an inviting space for our customers, and even a tasting room for our exclusive seasonal offerings. The opening marks a new chapter in Hydro's legacy, blending tradition with innovation to serve our loyal customers in exciting new ways.
